Villa Location and Vicinity

The Villa at Pelješka 2a is just steps from Dubrovnik's Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site celebrated for its medieval charm and rich history. Explore highlights like the Dubrovnik Cathedral, Rector's Palace, and Onofrio's Fountain.

Nearby, the Dubrovnik Cable Car offers breathtaking views of the city and sea. Visit Mount Srđ for panoramic views, dining, and the Homeland War Museum.
