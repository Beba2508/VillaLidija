About Villa

A short overview of your villa and its amenities.

Welcome to Majestic Villa Lidija, where comfort meets elegant simplicity. Located in the heart of Dubrovnik, the villa offers breathtaking views of the Adriatic and easy access to cultural landmarks, beaches, and restaurants.

  • Sea-view living room with terrace and infinity pool
  • Fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances
  • Dining area for 8 guests
  • 3 bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, double beds, and Smart TVs
  • Sauna in the top-floor master bedroom
  • Private gym and additional bathroom on the ground floor
  • High-speed Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and floor heating
